The Sound of Lips Manly

Lips Manly (frontman Will Corbin) transforms well-known pop songs from every decade into clever reinventions and mashups, backed by some of the finest musical talents in the region. The result is a stylish, upbeat show that looks sharp, grooves hard, and turns familiar songs into something audiences have never quite heard before.

Live Music on Florida's Emerald Coast

Lips Manly is a live band and musical brainchild of Will Corbin - based on Florida’s Emerald Coast, performing in Fort Walton Beach, Destin, Pensacola, and across Northwest Florida.

The band performs music from the 1920s through the 2000s, bringing high-energy live shows to bars, festivals, and private events throughout the Gulf Coast.
